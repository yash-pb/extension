YouTube Auto Pause & Resume

Automatically pauses and resumes YouTube videos when switching tabs or windows.

Instructions

  • Play a YouTube video.
  • Switch to another tab or window to pause the video.
  • Return to the YouTube tab or window to resume the video.

Instructions for disable this extension temporary

  • Right click on extension or there icon.
  • Then go to "This can read & change site data".
  • Select "When you click the extension".
  • So, if will turn off. If you want to start it click on extension icon.
